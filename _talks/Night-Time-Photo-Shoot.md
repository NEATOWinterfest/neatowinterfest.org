Enjoy taking photos of Trolleys at night. Sunsdown, polls up!

* **Sunset** is at `19:58` _(7:58pm)_
* **Civil** twilight: `19:58`
* **Nautical** twilight: `20:22`
* **Astronomical** twilight: `20:51`
